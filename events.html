EQUIPMENT

Only gloves are provided by USA Boxing, all other gear is your responsibility. Headgear must be a USA Boxing Masters approved headgear. Title, Ringside, Sting are the only 3 companies that are approved. You can use tape and gauze or traditional handwraps, you can not mix gauze and tape with traditional handwraps. You must use one or the other.

USA BOXERS & COACHES

you must be up-to-date with your membership with USA Boxing. Boxers must also have their medical up-to- date and yellow masters book with them in order to compete. Coaches must be up-to-date with their USA Boxing membership and passed the Green Level Coaching Test and Safesport. You’ll need to have your redbook that USA Boxing gives out to coaches. Without completion, you will not be able to corner your boxer. No exceptions will be made.

INTERNATIONAL BOXERS & COACHES

You must be in good standing and up-to-date with your Countries Federation’s membership Boxers must also have an up-to-date physical with those 45 years of age or older also having a passed exercise EKG with in the past 5 years. You must have your up-to-date card or book from your federation with you.

BOXERS WITHOUT COACHES

If you are traveling without a coach we will have coaches that will be ready to help corner you for your bout.

BOUT SHEETS

Bout Sheets will be posted 3 weeks before the show and bout sheets will be posted 1 week before the tournament. These will not be final until weigh-ins. People will drop out, change weight or not show up, this is why it’s crucial you are available both days to compete.
